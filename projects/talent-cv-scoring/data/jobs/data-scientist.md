# Job description — Data Scientist (proxy / sample)

**Role:** Data Scientist (illustrative sample, not a real posting)
**Location:** Remote-friendly · EU
**Languages:** English (fluent)

## Mission
Build and ship data/ML solutions for enterprise clients: from framing the problem to a deployed, evaluated model.

## Must-have
- 3+ years in data science or applied ML.
- Strong Python and the data stack (pandas, scikit-learn, or equivalent).
- Sound statistics and a habit of evaluating models honestly.
- Can communicate results to non-technical stakeholders.

## Nice-to-have
- Experience with LLMs / generative AI in production.
- MLOps (pipelines, monitoring, deployment).
- Domain depth in financial services or industry.

## What success looks like
Within 6 months: delivered a model into production with a clear evaluation, monitoring, and a stakeholder-readable readout of its limits.
