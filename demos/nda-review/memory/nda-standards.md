# NDA standards — Helios Consulting

Our house positions when reviewing or signing an NDA. These are defaults; Legal can override.

- **Term**: 2–3 years standard; survival of confidentiality obligations up to 5 years for
  trade secrets. **Never perpetual.**
- **Mutuality**: prefer mutual NDAs for exploratory discussions.
- **Required carveouts**: publicly available, prior possession, independently developed,
  rightfully received from a third party, required by law.
- **Never accept in an NDA**: non-solicitation, non-compete, exclusivity/standstill (outside
  M&A), hidden IP assignment or licence, liquidated-damages/penalty clauses.
- **Escalation**: any RED-flagged contract (a deal-breaker or material risk) must go to Legal
  for sign-off **before** it is returned to the client. The agent prepares the report; a
  human owns the decision.
