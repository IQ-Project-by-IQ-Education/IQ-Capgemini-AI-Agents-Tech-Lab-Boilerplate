# Job description — Engagement Manager (proxy / sample)

**Role:** Engagement Manager — consulting delivery (illustrative sample, not a real posting)
**Location:** Paris · client-site travel
**Languages:** English (fluent), French (fluent)

## Mission
Own the delivery of client engagements end to end: scope, team, timeline, budget, and client relationship.

## Must-have
- 6+ years in consulting, with 2+ leading delivery teams.
- Proven ownership of scope, budget, and client satisfaction.
- Strong stakeholder management up to executive level.
- Structured problem-solving and clear written communication.

## Nice-to-have
- Exposure to AI/data transformation programs.
- Sector depth (financial services, public sector, retail).
- Pre-sales / proposal experience.

## What success looks like
Within 6 months: led a multi-workstream engagement to on-time, on-budget delivery with a measurably satisfied client.
