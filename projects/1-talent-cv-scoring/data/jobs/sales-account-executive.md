# Job description — Sales Account Executive (proxy / sample)

**Role:** Sales Account Executive — B2B / enterprise sales (illustrative sample, not a real posting)
**Location:** Paris · field & remote · client travel
**Languages:** English (fluent), French (a plus)

## Mission
Own a portfolio of accounts end to end: prospect, qualify, run the sales cycle, and close — growing revenue against a quota.

## Must-have
- 3+ years in B2B / quota-carrying sales, with a track record of hitting target.
- Full-cycle selling: prospecting, discovery, negotiation, and closing.
- Pipeline and CRM discipline (e.g. Salesforce); accurate forecasting.
- Strong relationship-building and clear, persuasive communication.

## Nice-to-have
- Experience selling into a specific sector (retail, financial services, manufacturing).
- Account management / upsell & renewal exposure.
- Familiarity with a structured sales methodology (MEDDIC, Challenger, or similar).

## What success looks like
Within 6 months: built a qualified pipeline, closed against quota, and grew at least one key account through a measurable upsell.
